Welcome!

Beach Babies has grown to what it is today because of its simple philosophy:


To give infants and children plenty of love and security so they will thrive. Their beginning will be with them for a lifetime. Beach Babies wants to make it a great beginning!

The basics of our philosophy is respect for all children beginning with infancy. We involve the children in activities which concern them, help the children learn to find their own solutions and build security by teaching trust. We encourage and nurture each child to grow socially, physically, cognitively and emotionally. In this center, we know each child will gain confidence, self-respect and respect for others and the environment.

Our caretakers are facilitators, helping each child gain self esteem and confidence by learning individual skills. We emphasize curiosity, creativity, freedom of movement and communication. Our art and other curricula are open ended; we ask questions rather than impose personal beliefs.
